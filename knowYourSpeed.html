How to get most out of speed test


Test at different times of the day

Speeds can vary at different times of the day based on how many people are connected to the internet and what they are doing on it. To get a better understanding of the speeds in your homeMore


Check for other devices

Check no other people or devices are using the internet in your home or business when you run the test, as others using your internet connection can affect your results.


Connect directly to your modem

Connecting directly from your PC to the modem rather than using wifi will often give more accurate resutls. The device you run the test on can also affect your results.
